Products & Materials

Field fence is the most suitable for cattle farms. It uses high-tensile galvanised wire with graduated spacing engineered to contain large animals, withstand sustained pressure, and follow uneven terrain. It requires minimal maintenance and performs well in Zimbabwe's weather conditions.
Clearview fencing is the highest-security perimeter option. It is anti-climb, anti-cut welded mesh that is strong, visible, and modern in appearance. For maximum security, it is combined with razor wire topping — commonly used for commercial properties, schools, warehouses, and solar farms.
Diamond mesh (chain-link) is a cost-effective, flexible woven wire fencing used widely for residential, agricultural, and general perimeter applications. Clearview fencing is a rigid welded mesh with a smaller aperture that is harder to climb and cut — it provides significantly higher security at a higher cost. Clearview is generally preferred for commercial, institutional, and high-security applications.
