# Design QA — Razor Wire and Barbed Wire Product Pages

## Visual target

The established Azzar website design system: white-dominant surfaces, charcoal typography and dark process sections, Azzar green accents and CTAs, Syne-led display typography, restrained borders, large section spacing, and existing reveal motion.

The A-1 Fence barbed-wire page was used only as content-architecture inspiration: product benefits, applications, specification guidance, installation process, FAQs, and repeated enquiry opportunities.

## Checks

- Existing Azzar navigation, typography, color balance, buttons, spacing, cards, footer, and reveal classes were preserved.
- Razor Wire and Barbed Wire use separate product positioning, copy, imagery, applications, quoting inputs, and process content.
- No unverified product measurements, tensile ratings, standards, coil sizes, or roll lengths were published.
- Razor Wire does not use “concertina” in customer-facing or accessibility copy.
- All referenced product images load successfully.
- Desktop pages render without horizontal overflow at 1440px.
- Mobile pages collapse to one column without horizontal overflow at 390px.
- Five product-specific FAQs are present on each page.
- Browser console contains no errors or warnings during page checks.
