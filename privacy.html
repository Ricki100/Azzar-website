Privacy

Privacy and marketing data

This page explains the information the Azzar website may collect when you request a quote, contact the team or choose to load social media content.

Enquiries

When you submit an enquiry, Azzar uses the details you provide to understand and respond to your project. This can include your name, contact details, location, project type and the information you add about the site.

Marketing attribution

The website may retain campaign details such as UTM parameters and Google advertising click identifiers so Azzar can understand which marketing activity generated an enquiry.

Facebook content

The Facebook timeline is not loaded automatically. If you choose to display it, Facebook may receive technical information and set cookies under its own policies.

Your choices

You can contact Azzar by telephone or WhatsApp instead of using a website form. To ask about, correct or remove enquiry information, email sales@azzar.co.zw.

This practical notice must be reviewed by Azzar and aligned with the final CRM, analytics and retention configuration before launch.
