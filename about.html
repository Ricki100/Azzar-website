Our Story

Perimeter Security & Steel — Done Properly

Azzar Fencing and Steel is a Zimbabwean fencing and steel solutions company specialising in the supply and installation of high-quality perimeter security and structural steel products for residential, commercial, industrial, agricultural, mining, and institutional projects.

We combine practical industry experience with strong technical product knowledge to deliver solutions tailored to the unique requirements of each client and project — from a family home in Harare to a mining compound in the highlands.

The company focuses on delivering durable, reliable, and professionally installed solutions designed to provide long-term protection, security, and structural support across a wide range of environments.
